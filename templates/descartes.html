Module 01 — Foundational Doubt

Descartes' Stages of Doubt

In the Meditations (1641), Descartes systematically doubts everything that can be doubted — not to reach skepticism, but to find one indubitable foundation for knowledge.

Step through each stage of doubt. Watch which beliefs survive — and which are eliminated. Only one survives all three stages.
